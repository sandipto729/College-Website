Welcome to the Department of Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at NIT Durgapur is a hub of innovation, research, and learning. Established in 1960, the department is committed to excellence in education and creating a profound impact through cutting-edge research in the field of Computer Science and Information Technology.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a leader in computing education and research, driving technological innovation and contributing to the betterment of society through advancements in computer science.

Mission

  • To provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and engineering.
  • To foster innovation and creativity in research.
  • To collaborate with industry and academia to address global technological challenges.

Academic Programs

The department offers a variety of programs designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the evolving field of computer science.

  • B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering: A 4-year undergraduate program providing a comprehensive understanding of core computer science concepts.
  • M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering: A 2-year postgraduate program focusing on advanced research and practical applications.
  • Ph.D. Program: A research-focused program for students interested in pushing the boundaries of computer science.

Research & Collaboration

Our department is at the forefront of research, addressing various areas of computer science such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Data Science, and Software Engineering.

We collaborate with industries and universities globally to foster innovation and ensure our students and faculty stay ahead of the curve in emerging technologies.
